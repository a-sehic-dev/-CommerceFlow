What to import for accurate analytics

CommerceFlow calculates KPIs from your rows — not demo numbers. Missing columns or mismatched SKUs produce weak or misleading results. Upload all three files, then run analysis.

Open workspace guide

Sales Required columns

  • sku — same code as in products & inventory
  • revenue — line revenue (or unit_price × quantity)
  • quantity — units sold per row
  • sold_at — order / sale date (for trends & dead-stock rules)

Helpful: order_id, discount_amount, sales_channel, region

Products Required columns

  • sku — unique product code
  • title — product name (name / product_name also work)

Strongly recommended: price, cost (or margin_pct), category

Inventory Required columns

  • sku — matches sales & products
  • on_hand — stock on hand (quantity_on_hand / stock also work)

Helpful: warehouse, reserved, days_in_stock, inbound

Common mistakes (bad file → bad dashboard)

  • Different SKU formats across files (e.g. SKU-1 vs sku_1)
  • Sales without dates — velocity, dead inventory, and trends will be wrong
  • Products without cost/price — margin and profit leakage stay empty or weak
  • Only one file uploaded — analysis needs all three types selected
  • Duplicate SKUs in products — keep one row per SKU
