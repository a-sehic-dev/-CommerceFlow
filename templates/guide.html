Getting Started

Build an operational analytics workspace in four steps.

CommerceFlow turns sales, product, and inventory exports into dashboard KPIs, risk signals, alerts, and executive reporting workflows.

Upload Business Data Open dashboard
01

Importing datasets

Upload three operational files: sales, products, and inventory. CommerceFlow classifies supported CSV/XLSX exports from column headers.

  • Sales: orders, revenue, quantity, channel, sold date
  • Products: SKU, title, category, price, cost
  • Inventory: SKU, stock, reserved, aging, warehouse
02

Running analysis

Click Run Your Analysis and select one import for each dataset type. KPIs stay empty until you run it.

For accurate results, you need:

  • Products — SKU + product name (price, cost, category help)
  • Sales — revenue per row + order date + SKU (order ID and quantity help)
  • Inventory — SKU + stock on hand
  • Same SKU across all three files (matching codes, not different formats)

CommerceFlow calculates from your numbers. Rules like low stock (≤14 days cover) or dead inventory (180+ days without sales) are standard thresholds — not demo data.

03

Dashboard modules

Use the sidebar modules to inspect the operational story behind the dashboard numbers.

  • Executive Overview for KPIs and trends
  • Inventory Health for low stock, overstock, and dead inventory
  • Alerts and Profit Leakage for priority operational actions
04

Exporting reports

Generate structured management exports for analysis handoff, founder updates, or operational review.

  • Excel reports with executive summaries
  • CSV/JSON exports for downstream workflows
  • Traceable metrics from selected datasets
